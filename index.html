I am a sophomore at Brigham Young University studying Information Systems, with career interests in product management and strategy consulting. What draws me to product management is the opportunity to build features and products that people actually enjoy using, whether that is a tool that saves someone time or an experience that genuinely delights them. What draws me to both fields is the style of thinking they demand.
